Our Practice

We operate from Argentina, providing services both locally and internationally. We are a design company involved in the delivery of Architecture, Urban Design & Interior Design providing services in all aspects of Architecture from Inception to Delivery, as Design Architects to Architects’ of Record. Client satisfaction is our key objective. We produce memorable buildings and places that are contextual and sustainable. The practice was founded in 2020 by its two directors, Marie Valiño and Sofie Maurici. We are a Team of Architects, Technologists, Interior Designers and Support Staff, whose passion and skills come together to produce designs of very high standards. Our core team of “Associates” ensures the proper implementation of ASL Strategic Vision & Strategic Plan
